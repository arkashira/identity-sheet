# Business Model Canvas for Identity-Sheet

## Customer Segments
- Small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs) that rely heavily on spreadsheets for data management.
- Financial analysts and accountants who need to minimize errors in their reports.
- Project managers who require simplified maintenance of complex project data.
- Educational institutions using spreadsheets for student data management and grading.

## Value Propositions
- Identity-based referencing reduces errors in spreadsheet formulas, enhancing data integrity.
- Simplified maintenance of spreadsheets, saving time and reducing frustration for users.
- User-friendly interface that allows for easy adoption without extensive training.
- Increased productivity through streamlined data management processes.

## Channels
- Direct sales through the official website and GitHub repository.
- Partnerships with software resellers and IT consultants.
- Online marketing campaigns targeting spreadsheet users on platforms like LinkedIn and Reddit.
- Webinars and tutorials showcasing the benefits and functionality of Identity-Sheet.

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated customer support via email and chat to assist with onboarding and troubleshooting.
- Community forums for users to share tips, tricks, and best practices.
- Regular updates and newsletters to keep customers informed about new features and improvements.
- Feedback loops to gather user input for continuous product enhancement.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model: Monthly fee of $15 (approx. 500 THB) per user for premium features.
- One-time license fee of $150 (approx. 5,000 THB) for perpetual access to the software.
- Enterprise solutions with customized pricing based on user count and additional features.
- Affiliate programs for partners who refer new customers.

## Key Resources
- Development team skilled in spreadsheet software and identity-based referencing technology.
- Marketing team to promote the product and engage with potential customers.
- Customer support team to assist users and maintain satisfaction.
- Partnerships with educational institutions for pilot programs and feedback.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and enhancement of the Identity-Sheet software.
- Marketing campaigns to raise awareness and attract new users.
- User training and support to ensure successful adoption of the product.
- Gathering and analyzing user feedback for product improvements.

## Key Partners
- Software resellers and IT consultants who can promote and sell Identity-Sheet.
- Educational institutions for pilot programs and case studies.
- Online platforms (e.g., LinkedIn, Reddit) for targeted advertising and community engagement.
- Influencers and thought leaders in the spreadsheet and data management space.

## Cost Structure
- Development costs: Salaries for developers and software maintenance (estimated $200,000/year).
- Marketing expenses: Online advertising, content creation, and promotional events (estimated $50,000/year).
- Customer support costs: Salaries for support staff and tools for managing customer inquiries (estimated $30,000/year).
- Operational costs: Hosting, software licenses, and administrative expenses (estimated $20,000/year).
