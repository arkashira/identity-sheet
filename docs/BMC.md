# Business Model Canvas – **identity‑sheet**

| **Building Block** | **Details** |
|--------------------|-------------|
| **Value Proposition** | • **Error‑proof spreadsheets** – Replace fragile A1‑style cell references with immutable, human‑readable identifiers (e.g., `Revenue.Q1`). <br>• **Self‑documenting formulas** – Formulas read like natural language, reducing onboarding time and maintenance overhead. <br>• **Version‑safe refactoring** – Insert, delete, or move rows/columns without breaking dependent calculations. <br>• **Audit‑ready** – Automatic change‑log of identifier mappings for compliance‑heavy industries (finance, pharma, logistics). |
| **Customer Segments** | 1. **Finance & Accounting teams** – CFO offices, FP&A analysts, auditors who rely on large, complex workbooks. <br>2. **Data‑driven product teams** – Product managers & analysts building KPI dashboards in spreadsheets. <br>3. **SMBs & startups** – Teams that cannot afford full‑blown BI tools but need reliable spreadsheets. <br>4. **Enterprise IT & Governance** – Companies with strict change‑control policies looking to modernize legacy spreadsheet processes. |
| **Channels** | • **Direct SaaS portal** – Web‑app with single‑sign‑on (SSO) integration (SAML, OAuth). <br>• **Marketplace integrations** – Add‑on for Google Workspace Marketplace and Microsoft AppSource (Google Sheets & Excel). <br>• **Partner‑led implementation** – System integrators (e.g., Deloitte, Accenture) bundle identity‑sheet in digital‑transformation projects. <br>• **Open‑source community** – Free CLI/SDK on GitHub for power‑users; drives adoption and feedback. |
| **Revenue Streams** | 1. **Subscription SaaS** – Tiered plans (Starter $9/user/mo, Professional $25/user/mo, Enterprise $45/user/mo). <br>2. **Enterprise licensing** – Unlimited seats, on‑premise deployment, dedicated support (annual contract). <br>3. **Add‑on Marketplace fees** – 15 % revenue share with Google/Microsoft for marketplace sales. <br>4. **Professional services** – Migration, custom identifier schema design, training workshops (hourly or fixed‑price). |
| **Cost Structure** | • **Cloud infrastructure** – Compute & storage (AWS/GCP) for real‑time collaboration and audit logs. <br>• **Engineering & product** – Salaries for dev, QA, UX, and AI‑assisted identifier parsing. <br>• **Compliance & security** – SOC 2, ISO 27001 audits, data‑encryption tooling. <br>• **Sales & marketing** – Content, webinars, partner commissions, marketplace listing fees. <br>• **Support & success** – Tier‑1 help desk, dedicated CSMs for Enterprise accounts. |
| **Key Resources** | • **Identity‑sheet codebase** – Core engine (Rust/Go) that maps identifiers to cell addresses, diff engine, and API layer. <br>• **AI‑assisted migration tooling** – Trained on the company’s 7 M+ paired datasets to auto‑convert existing A1 formulas to identifiers. <br>• **Domain expertise** – Knowledge of spreadsheet error patterns (from QA coverage analytics product). <br>• **Platform integrations** – SDKs for Google Sheets, Microsoft Excel, and CSV/Excel import/export. <br>• **Brand & community** – Open‑source contributors, early‑adopter user group. |
| **Key Activities** | 1. **Product development** – Core engine, UI/UX, marketplace add‑ons, AI conversion pipelines. <br>2. **Integration & certification** – Maintain compatibility with Google Sheets & Excel APIs, pass marketplace security reviews. <br>3. **Customer onboarding & migration** – Automated scripts to convert legacy workbooks, training webinars. <br>4. **Compliance & security** – Continuous monitoring, vulnerability scanning, audit‑log retention. <br>5. **Growth & partnership** – Co‑marketing with spreadsheet platforms, enable reseller programs. |
| **Key Partners** | • **Google (Workspace)** – Marketplace distribution, API access, joint go‑to‑market. <br>• **Microsoft (Office 365)** – Excel add‑in certification, Azure hosting credits. <br>• **Data‑integration platforms** – Zapier, Tray.io (for triggering updates from external systems). <br>• **System integrators & consultancies** – Implement identity‑sheet in regulated environments. <br>• **Open‑source community** – Contributors to the CLI/SDK, bug triage, feature suggestions. |

### Narrative Summary

**identity‑sheet** tackles a universal pain point: spreadsheet errors caused by fragile positional references. By introducing immutable, human‑readable identifiers, the product eliminates a major source of financial and operational risk, delivering measurable cost savings (estimated $10‑$30 k per 100‑person finance team per year). The solution is delivered as a SaaS platform with native add‑ins for the two dominant spreadsheet ecosystems, ensuring low friction adoption while also offering an on‑premise enterprise option for highly regulated customers.

Revenue is primarily subscription‑based, with a clear upgrade path from a low‑cost starter tier (for SMBs) to high‑margin enterprise contracts that include premium support and custom migration services.
